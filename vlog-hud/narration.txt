Microservices versus monolithic. Quick comparison.

Monoliths ship fast: one codebase, one deploy, simple ops. The downside? Scaling means scaling everything, even parts that don't need it.

Microservices scale independently. Different teams, different stacks, different release cadences. The cost: network latency, distributed tracing, and operational overhead that grows with every service.

Most teams should start with a monolith. Split when the seams hurt.
